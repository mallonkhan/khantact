Proud Fools

Founded in 2013 by Mallon Khan, Proud Fools ran until 2018 after Mallon relocated to Los Angeles. Economics and Humble Genus were written, recorded, and produced by Mallon Khan in NYC from 2009-2013. Keko and Michal joined in 2016.

Pretty As Shit credits: Mallon Khan on guitar, vocals, co-writing, and production; Michal Olowina on bass, co-writing, and co-production; Keko on drums.
